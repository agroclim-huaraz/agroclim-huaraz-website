Two AgroClim-Huaraz contributions at EGU2020

#shareEGU20

Posted by Cornelia Klein on May 5, 2020

Our project is well represented at the EGU session about Recent progress in understanding the hydro-climate system of the Andes: Physical processes and resources for Andean populations, to be “chatted” on Tuesday 05 May, 14:00-15:45.

Lorenz will present first results from field observations with the EC station and the EcoBot (link to abstract and presentation), and I will present new results about the relationship of rainfall and convection in the Rio Santa Basin to large-scale circulation factors (link to abstract and presentation).

Looking forward for exiting discussions this afternoon!
