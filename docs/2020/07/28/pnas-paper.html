Cornelia Klein helps to develop early warning system for megastorms

New publication in PNAS

Posted by Fabien Maussion on July 28, 2020

AgroClim Huaraz project scientist Cornelia Klein led a new study, published recently in the journal PNAS. This groundbreaking research will make it easier to predict the path of some of the world’s most powerful storms, enabling communities to better protect themselves from severe flooding.

Here a a few news outlets talking about it:

  • Verbessertes Frühwarnsystem für Megastürme entwickelt (Die Presse, in German)
  • Innsbrucker Forscher entwickelten Frühwarnsystem für „Megastürme“ (Tiroler Tageszeitung, in German)
